What is an escape room?

Our Escape Rooms are designed for those, who love solving puzzles, finding clues and feeling the thrill of an adventure. You do not need any special skills, just creativity and curiosity! This is a great way to spend a day with family or friends while having tons of fun!

Use your wits to find hints, solve a problem and get the key to your freedom, or save the world! You must escape a room filled with a series of challenging puzzles, all within sixty minutes. Time is limited! The best advice? Hurry!

If you have a rich imagination and are looking for a challenge, this game is for you. It’s great for family bonding or for team-building. Throw a birthday party, have a kids’ playdate, sign up for some high quality team building or even take a date to our Escape Room. You will have a memorable and unique time. Make up a team and go just for the fun of it. Everyone will be satisfied. That is guaranteed!

What you gain

Special event

Are you having a birthday party or a get-together with friends? Make the day special. Instead of filling up your stomachs at a restaurant, feed your imagination. Instead of an ordinary birthday present, give the long-lasting fun of an Escape Room adventure. The game will be interesting and exciting for everyone.

Love puzzles?

It is never too late to explore and have fun. We learn something new whenever we face obstacles. Go for it! The Escape Room is a delightful way to spend your time, actively exploring, gaining new experience, and exercising your ingenuity.

Fun for all

Escape Room activities are fun and interesting for each individual. Kids, teenagers, and adults will find tasks that appeal to them. The activities offer everyone the opportunity to improve problem solving skills, strengthen logical thinking and reaction speed. And remember, these are all just part of the game!

Team building

The key to success is a teamwork. While trying to survive in an Escape room you will get to know each other much better; you will be able to build relationships and strengthen mutual understanding within a group. Intense cooperation will lead your team to a greater achievements!
