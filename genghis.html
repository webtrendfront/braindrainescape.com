Genghis khan's
treasures
2-5 players Age: 6+ Rating: 9.9  
Book now

Over 700 years ago one of the most frightening conquerors in history, powerful Genghis Khan – the Khan of Mongol Empire – was buried in a tomb along with all his treasures. Since then, scores of people have disappeared trying to seize Genghis Khan’s wealth.

You have found your way inside Genghis Khan’s tomb, which means you have become another obstacle in the headhunters’ way. They have been chasing the cursed treasure to this day and nothing can stop them. Watch out for others and for yourself! Greed and ancient curses can turn people into cold-blooded murderers.

In 60 minutes you will run out of oxygen and the tomb will close, burying you inside. You need to solve each of the puzzles and exit the tomb as quickly as possible. Your time is limited – don`t waste it! Get moving!
